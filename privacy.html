Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Headless Oracle is designed with architectural data minimization. We believe the safest way to handle user data is not to collect it in the first place.

1. Data We Collect

We strictly limit data collection to what is necessary for operational security and verifying Liability Receipts:

  • API Key Identifier: To authenticate your X-Oracle-Key header and enforce rate limits.
  • Request Metadata: The receipt_id, MIC code queried, and response status. These are stored in our audit logs to prove operational integrity.
  • Email Address: Only if provided for account management, used strictly for billing and critical security alerts (e.g., "Key Rotation").
  • IP Address: Logged temporarily for rate-limiting and DDoS protection, then purged.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

To preserve our status under the Publisher's Exclusion doctrine, we do not touch financial data:

  • We do NOT collect portfolio holdings, trading positions, or account balances.
  • We do NOT collect wallet addresses or private keys.
  • We do NOT track your browsing history or use advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Data

Your data is used for two purposes only: Service Delivery (letting you query the API) and Security (detecting abuse or proof of attestation for dispute resolution).

4. Data Retention

Operational audit logs are retained for 90 days. After this period, they are permanently deleted. You may request earlier deletion of your account metadata by contacting support.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns: security@headlessoracle.com
